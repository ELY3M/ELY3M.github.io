Atmosphere Setup for beginners

What you need: 
1. exploitable switch - check your serial with this site at https://ismyswitchpatched.com
2. a computer or a laptop
3. RCM jig - you can find them on ebay.com, esty.com, or amazon.com.  they are $5 to $6 each.
4. 128 gb sd card for backing up nand and setting up emunand and running atmosphere.    
5. C to A USB cable for hooking up your switch to your computer.

There is two option of setting up atmosphere on your switch.
1. creating emunand and using emunand with atmosphere (lowest ban risk on your sysnand and keeping your sysnand clean)
2. setting up atmosphere and running it over your sysnand.


How to get your Nintendo Switch in RCM mode.
1. remove the joycon from right side.  
2. put RCM jig in the right joycon rails.  
3.  make sure it is way in the rail.   
4. press and hold volume + then press power button.  the switch screen should stay black.  
5. plug your switch to your computer usb slot with your usb cable.   





Setting up TegraRcmGui on your computer (windows)
1. get TegraRcmGui zip from https://github.com/eliboa/TegraRcmGUI/releases
2. unzip to your desired location on your drive. 
3. open TegraRcmGui and go to settings tab and click to install drivers so your computer can see your Switch when it is connected to your computer.

If you want to run clean/fresh Atmosphere
follow these steps

1. Backup your Nintendo and emuMMC (if you have this folder) folder on your sd card to your pc.
2. Format your sd card as fat32 with guiformat app. - if you get error. you need to kill explorer.exe with taskmgr then format then restart explorer.exe
3. download the latest Atmosphere zip file and extract to the root of your sd card https://github.com/Atmosphere-NX/Atmosphere/releases
4. download fusee-primary.bin, this is the payload you will use to boot your Switch. (It will go in your tegrarcumgui, on your dongle, on your phone)
5. download Sigpatches from here and extract to the root of your sd card.
6. copy your Nintendo and emuMMC (if you have this folder) folder back to your sd card and put your sd card back in your Switch.
7. get your switch in RCM mode then inject fusee-primary.bin
8. Hold R while tapping the Album Icon to enter the Homebrew Menu.
